Your imaginative child: a fantastic toy designer!

Our friendly Makie Creator can help your child turn that creative spark into a unique doll with a fabulous style and personality. We’ll make it into a stunning, one-of-a-kind 10” doll, and ship it directly to your home within two weeks.

Sure, Makies look amazing, but they’ve also got brains, and heart, and Can-Do attitude. For Makies, science and technology are awesome tools that empower them to change the world, and they love using their skills to make life better and happier for everyone.

Help your child discover and express limitless creativity. In addition to a wide range of ready-made accessories, Makies can be customised with easy-to-use CREATE-IT KITS. Budding Makers can learn how to make and style their own gorgeous fashion, build and customise furniture, and even 3D-print their own accessories.

Manufactured using cutting-edge technology, Makies are designed and assembled in London, England, and ship to wherever you are in the world. Local production means we only make exactly what we need, where we need it, which helps reduce our impact on the environment.

We want you to be perfectly happy with your Makie, so we offer a no-fuss 30 day money back guarantee.
